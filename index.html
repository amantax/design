Frequently Asked Questions – FAQs

Can I file an Income Tax Return using Taxmanager.in services? ▼
Yes! You can file your ITR quickly and securely with TaxManager’s guided filing system. Simply follow our steps and upload the required documents.
What are the steps for filing an Income Tax Return? ▼
1. Register/Login to TaxManager
2. Enter personal + financial details
3. Upload Form 16 / other docs
4. Preview and file your return
How can I create my ITR? ▼
Simply fill your basic details, choose ITR type, and TaxManager will auto-generate your return. Review and file in one click.
How to fill Tax Return details? ▼
You will be guided section by section for each income, deduction, and bank account. Smart hints make it easy.
How to fill Income Sources Details? ▼
Add sources like salary, rental income, capital gains, etc., with TaxManager’s intuitive inputs. You can also import from Form 26AS.
